Secondary Marketplace

Our secondary marketplace gives you the ability to liquidate your shares at a time that is right for you. No more being stuck in long-term investments that give you little to no control over when or how you can sell.

Democratize Real Estate Investing

PadShares seeks to democratize real estate investing by lowering the threshold for getting started and/or growing your existing portfolio. Let’s face it, past traditional methods of investing in real estate can be hard for the vast majority of people.

Finding deals, knowing how to analyze the numbers, estimating repairs, coming up with money down, managing tenants or even staging a house to sell; these are all skills an investor must have in order to succeed.

With PadShares, we make things easy by allowing you to buy a few shares or a thousand shares of expertly vetted and managed investment properties. In just 5 minutes, beginning investors can buy into their first deal or experienced investors can expand their portfolio. Don’t wait.

Traditional real estate investing can be hard. With PadShares, you get the benefits of cash flow, appreciation, diversification and liquidity without the hard work. Get started now with as little as $100.

How It Works

1

Sign up and download the app

2

Browse

Browse properties and choose which property you want to buy fractional deeds of.

3

Buy shares

Buy as few or as many shares as you feel comfortable with.

4

Vote

Each fractional deed carries one vote. The more deeds you own, the more votes you can cast. Voting is required at least quarterly in order to receive returns.

5

Collect

Quarterly earnings are deposit right into you account.

6

Grow your portfolio

Continue to build your real estate portfolio buy buying as few or as many shares across a diversified number of properties.

7

Resell

Sell your deeds on your terms when you are ready using our secondary marketplace. Or you can sell to a friend, family member or associate and then conduct the sale through our secure app
